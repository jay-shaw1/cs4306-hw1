# CS4306 Assignment 1
In this project, we tackled a prominent problem when it comes to recruitment: how does one make the most ideal matching between recruiters and recruitees based on preferences?
Our solution is a modified variation of the Gale-Shapley Stable Matching algorithm aimed at addressing this very issue. 
> [!NOTE]
> It is also worth mentioning that (1) in our version of the algorithm, hospitals take on the role of recruiters and medical students are the recruitees, (2) hospitals can recruit more than one student.
